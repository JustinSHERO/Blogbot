For a budget-friendly alternative to drywall, consider using plywood with a waterproofing sealant. This option is perfect for basements, garages, and other areas that are prone to moisture damage. The plywood provides a sturdy base, while the sealant protects against water damage and mold growth. Plus, you can easily paint or cover the plywood to match your desired look. Gypsum Panels

Another cost-effective alternative to drywall is gypsum panels. These panels are made of recycled materials and have a natural resistance to fire, mold, and insects. They are also easy to install and can be painted or covered with wallpaper for a finished look. Plus, gypsum panels are lightweight and have excellent soundproofing properties.
